the project's name, logos, and identity, which are **not** open source and remain
the property of the maintainer.

## What you may do

- Run, study, modify, and redistribute the **code** under the AGPL-3.0.
- Refer to the project by name to give credit or describe compatibility
  ("based on Mandum Rimba", "a fork of Mandum Rimba"). This is nominative use.
- Link to [mandumrimba.org](https://mandumrimba.org) and this repository.

## What you may not do without written permission

- Use the **Mandum Rimba** / **Lam Rimba** name or logos as the name or branding
  of **your own** fork, product, app, or service.
- Use the marks in a way that implies official status, endorsement, or
  affiliation.
- Register the marks, or confusingly similar marks/domains, anywhere.

## Deploying a derivative (attribution)

If you publicly deploy a derivative of this code (a website, app, or service):

1. **Rebrand it.** Use your own name and logo, not Mandum Rimba's.
2. **Keep the credit.** Display a visible, working credit on the site,
   **"Based on Mandum Rimba, https://mandumrimba.org"**, and preserve the
   copyright notices in the source (see [NOTICE](./NOTICE)).

This keeps the code free while keeping the identity clearly the maintainer's.
