# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Mandum Rimba to be a welcoming place to contribute, whatever your
background or experience. We pledge to keep participation in this project a
respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Things that help:

- Being kind, patient, and respectful.
- Assuming good faith and giving constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the people it serves.
- Backing claims with sources, in keeping with the project's editorial values.

Things that are not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or personal or political attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or review.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, and discussions) and
when someone is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the
maintainer on Threads at [@r.rasyidi](https://www.threads.com/@r.rasyidi).
Reports will be handled discreetly. Maintainers may remove comments, reject
contributions, or block accounts that breach this code.

This is a small project run in good faith. Treat each other well and we will all
get further.
